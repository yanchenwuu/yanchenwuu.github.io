About

I am a researcher working on interfacial phenomena, with a focus on droplet wetting, capillary transport, geometrically confined nucleation, and bioinspired micro-/nanomanufacturing. My research aims to understand how surface geometry, wettability, and thermodynamic driving forces regulate liquid motion, phase transition, and material deposition across micro- and nanoscale confined spaces.

Research Interests

  • Droplet wetting and capillary phenomena
  • Geometrically confined nucleation
  • Bioinspired structured interfaces
  • Phase-field modeling of complex interfacial morphologies
  • Micro-/nanomanufacturing and precision printing
